Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Log in to your patient portal to book an in-person or telehealth consultation. Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance.
How can I request a prescription refill?
You can request a refill by logging into your patient portal and following the instructions under prescription requests.
How do I access my medical records?
Your medical records are accessible through the patient portal. Log in to view your records.
Is my personal health information secure?
Absolutely. We adhere to strict security protocols to protect all patient data and medical records.
Where is CareWell Clinic located?

What is your payment and billing policy?
We take all major credit cards, and we also offer payment plans to help you pay for your medical care.
How do I need to pay for services?
We accept various payment methods. Please check with our billing department for details.
How do you maintain confidentiality?
We have strict confidentiality measures in place, ensuring that your personal and health information remains secure at all times.
